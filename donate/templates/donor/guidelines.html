Guidelines for Donors


1. Post genuine items for donation,i.e. items that cannot be used should not be posted

2. Look at the items required before posting any donation.

3. Give genuine personal information and give complete address with phone number

4. Be kind to volunteers who come to collect your items

5. You can always choose to be a volunteer by clicking "join as volunteer" button and follow the instruction there after, do it only if you are willing to be volunteer

6. You can join any events organized by NGO's by looking at upcoming events
