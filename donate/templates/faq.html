FAQ's

What is Letusdonate?

Letusdonate is a Non-Profit network that facilitates interaction between donors and NGO's from different parts of the country. It is place where donors can make donation to variety of charity and for the good cause,aslo NGO's can reach out to people for organizing charity events and also get in contact with volunteers.

Who can join Letusdonate?

Anyone with valid e-mail id can join Letusdonate

Where can i donate an item?

After logging in as a donar you can just create your donation as per the guidelines and items required,you should also specify your details asked

What are the guidelines for donation?

1. Post genuine items for donation,i.e. items that cannot be used should not be posted

2. Look at the items required before posting any donation.

3. Give genuine personal information and give complete address with phone number

4. Be kind to volunteers who come to collect your items

5. You can always choose to be a volunteer by clicking "join as volunteer" button and follow the instruction there after,do it only if you are willing to be volunteer

6. You can join any events organized by NGO's by looking at upcoming events

I am a donor but can i volunteer for any events?

Absolutely,there is join as volunteer option available once you login as donor,just follow the steps there after

Can any new NGO join Letusdonate?

Yes!you just need to create NGO account or else you can contact us for any details.
